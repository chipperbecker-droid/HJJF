Practice Area

Municipal Law

Municipal law counsel for Delaware towns, cities, counties, boards, agencies, and other governmental entities.

Contact Our Firm View Delaware Practice Areas

Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher provides municipal law counsel to local government entities and serves as trusted legal advisor to municipalities. The firm assists towns, cities, counties, boards, agencies, and governmental entities with legal issues involving public administration, regulatory compliance, ordinances, land use, employment, and litigation.

The firm advises on Freedom of Information Act compliance, land use and zoning matters, municipal election issues, risk avoidance, liability management, public-sector employment concerns, and local government procedures. The firm also drafts ordinances involving zoning, building codes, public safety, environmental protection, business licensing, and revenue matters.

The firm represents municipalities in litigation, bond closings, employment disputes, appeals, and general civil matters. Its experience representing both governmental entities and private clients in municipal-law matters provides useful perspective in resolving disputes and advising decision-makers.

How We Help

  • Town solicitor and municipal counsel services
  • FOIA, elections, employment, and risk management
  • Ordinance drafting and regulatory frameworks
  • Municipal litigation, appeals, and bond closings
