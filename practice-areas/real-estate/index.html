Practice Area

Real Estate

Delaware real estate counsel for residential and commercial closings, title review, purchase agreements, financing, foreclosure, refinancing, and property disputes.

Contact Our Firm View Delaware Practice Areas

Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher assists clients with residential and commercial real estate transactions and disputes. Whether a client is a first-time homebuyer, seller, lender, developer, investor, landlord, or tenant, the firm provides legal guidance designed to protect property interests and support smooth transactions.

The firm handles real estate closings, contract review and negotiation, title examination, title insurance, due diligence, document preparation, escrow matters, foreclosure representation, and residential or commercial refinancings.

When disputes arise, the firm represents clients in real estate litigation involving breach of contract, boundary disputes, easements, title issues, quiet title actions, landlord-tenant litigation, construction defects, and other property-related claims.

How We Help

  • Residential and commercial closings
  • Contract review, title examination, and title insurance
  • Foreclosures and refinancings
  • Boundary, easement, title, lease, and construction disputes
