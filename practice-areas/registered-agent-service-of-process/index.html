Delaware Registered Agent FAQ

What does a Delaware registered agent do?

A Delaware registered agent provides an official point of contact for an entity and may receive service of process or official notices as allowed by law and the engagement terms.

Who may need a Delaware registered agent?

Delaware entities and some out-of-state businesses registered to do business in Delaware may need a registered agent with an appropriate Delaware presence.

Can the firm accept service of process?

The firm may accept service of process where appropriate. Businesses and counsel should contact the firm first to confirm the entity, authority, documents, and timing.
