Practice Area

Trusts, Wills & Estates

Delaware estate planning and estate administration guidance for wills, trusts, powers of attorney, living wills, probate, and incapacity planning.

Contact Our Firm Explore Services

Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher helps clients plan for the future through wills, trusts, estate planning, and incapacity planning. Thoughtful planning helps ensure that a client's wishes are honored, assets are protected, and loved ones are cared for if incapacity or death occurs.

The firm prepares Last Wills and Testaments, helps clients designate beneficiaries, appoint executors, nominate guardians for minor children, and establish specific gifts or charitable bequests. The firm also advises on trusts, including revocable living trusts, irrevocable trusts, special needs trusts, testamentary trusts, and charitable trusts where appropriate.

The firm prepares durable powers of attorney, advance health-care directives, and living wills so trusted agents can manage financial, legal, and medical decisions if a client becomes unable to do so. These documents can help avoid confusion, family disputes, and unnecessary court involvement.
