The Dover Office, still at 225 South State Street, occupies all of the buildings between historic Wesley Church and North Street.

With a large in-house law library and direct access to online research, the attorneys and paralegals can readily access the statutes and latest case decisions needed to provide sound legal counsel.

Meetings and depositions are facilitated by three large conference rooms, and real estate closings are handled by the firm's real estate department, which occupies an entire wing of the complex.

Situated less than two miles from the Kent County Courthouse, the Delaware Supreme Court, the Kent County Administration building, the Delaware Corporations Department, the Delaware General Assembly, and Dover City Hall, the office is conveniently located to serve clients whose matters require access to those institutions.

Dover attorneys include Katrina K. Doran, D. Barrett Edwards IV, Angelica M. Mamani, Michael G. Rushe, and Fred A. Townsend, III.

The Rehoboth Beach Office, at 309 Rehoboth Avenue, is often used by attorneys from the Dover office to meet with Sussex County clients.

Located on the main street of The Nation's Summer Capital, the Rehoboth Beach office provides a wide range of legal services and is especially known for what the resort area demands most: real estate, condominium, and homeowners association matters.

The office is centrally located in the heart of the Rehoboth real estate and banking sector and is only 30 minutes from the Sussex County Courthouse in Georgetown.

Rehoboth attorneys include James Pahl Becker, Richard E. Berl Jr., Christopher M. Holmon, Chad C. Meredith, Daniel Patrick Myers II, Fred A. Townsend, III, and Robert V. Witsil, Jr.
