About Us

One of Southern Delaware's oldest and largest law firms, Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher traces its roots to the 1950s and has served Delaware clients since 1975.

Contact Our Firm View Delaware Practice Areas

Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher, LLC is one of Southern Delaware's oldest and largest law firms, tracing its roots back to the 1950s when David P. Buckson, later Attorney General and Lt. Governor of Delaware and a Family Court Judge, and George R. Wright, later Deputy Attorney General and Delaware Superior Court Judge, shared offices at 225 South State Street in Dover. William S. Hudson joined them around 1960, and the firm's formal origin followed in 1975 as Hudson, Jones and Jaywork.

Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher is a full-service Delaware law firm serving individuals, families, businesses, property owners, associations, and public-sector clients from offices in Dover and Rehoboth Beach.

The firm provides legal counsel and representation across automobile accidents and other personal injury matters, civil litigation, business law, residential and commercial real estate transactions, criminal law, municipal and zoning law, domestic relations, creditor relations, wills and estates, and administrative law.

The firm recognizes that the practice of law is a service business built on quality work, careful judgment, responsive service, trust, and durable client relationships.

The firm offers a variety of billing arrangements depending on the nature of the matter and client preferences, including hourly rates, flat fees, contingent fees, and agreed-result hourly rates.
