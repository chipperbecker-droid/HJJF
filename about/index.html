About Us

One of southern Delaware's oldest and largest law firms, Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher traces its roots to the 1950s and has served Delaware clients since 1975.

The firm provides legal counsel and representation across personal injury, civil litigation, business law, real estate, criminal law, municipal and zoning law, domestic relations, wills, estates, and administrative matters.

The firm recognizes that the practice of law is a service business built on careful judgment, personal attention, and durable client relationships.

The firm offers a variety of billing arrangements depending on the nature of the matter and client preferences, including hourly rates, flat fees, contingent fees, and agreed-result hourly rates.
