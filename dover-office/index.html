Dover Office

The Dover office remains at 225 South State Street, close to Delaware courts, government offices, and civic institutions.

Contact Our Firm View Delaware Practice Areas

The Dover Office, still at 225 South State Street, occupies all of the buildings between historic Wesley Church and North Street.

With a large in-house law library and direct access to online research, the attorneys and paralegals can readily access the statutes and latest case decisions needed to provide sound legal counsel.

Meetings and depositions are facilitated by three large conference rooms, and real estate closings are handled by the firm's real estate department, which occupies an entire wing of the complex.

Situated less than two miles from the Kent County Courthouse, the Delaware Supreme Court, the Kent County Administration building, the Delaware Corporations Department, the Delaware General Assembly, and Dover City Hall, the office is conveniently located for clients with matters in Dover, Kent County, and throughout Delaware.
