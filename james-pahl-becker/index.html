Attorney Profile

James Pahl Becker

Member

Rehoboth Beach Office

James Pahl Becker (Jay) was born in Ft. Worth, Texas. He is a graduate of The University of the South, Sewanee, Tennessee (B.A. 1994/cum laude) and the University of Texas School of Law, Austin, Texas (J.D. 1997). Mr. Becker is admitted to practice before the Delaware Bar. He is a member of the Sussex County and Delaware State Bar Associations. Jay served on the board of the Rehoboth Beach / Dewey Beach Chamber of Commerce for 23 years, serving as its chairman in 2017 and 2018. Jay currently serves on the board of Delaware Wild Lands, Inc., Delaware’s largest not-for-profit, non-governmental landowner and conservation organization.

Jay is primarily a transactional attorney, whose areas of practice include real estate transactions (both residential and commercial), wills, small business formations, commercial transactions, including an active practice before the Office of the Alcoholic Beverage Control Commissioner, and other civil matters. He is a title agent for Old Republic Title Insurance Company and First American Title Insurance Company.

Mr. Becker lives near Rehoboth with his wife and attack poodle. He enjoys spending as much time as he can with his two grown children, as well as reading history.
