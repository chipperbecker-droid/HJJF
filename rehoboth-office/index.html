Hudson, Jones, Jaywork & Fisher's Rehoboth Beach office serves clients throughout Sussex County from 309 Rehoboth Avenue, a central location in Delaware's coastal real estate and business corridor.

The office provides a convenient meeting location for clients in Rehoboth Beach, Lewes, Dewey Beach, Bethany Beach, Georgetown, Millville, Ocean View, and surrounding Sussex County communities. The firm's attorneys assist clients with a wide range of Delaware legal matters, including real estate, condominium and homeowners association issues, land use, business matters, estate planning and administration, and civil litigation.

Attorneys from both the Rehoboth Beach and Dover offices use this location to meet with Sussex County clients and provide practical legal guidance close to where clients live, work, and invest.

Located approximately 30 minutes from the Sussex County Courthouse in Georgetown, the Rehoboth Beach office offers convenient access for individuals, families, businesses, developers, lenders, and community associations across southern Delaware.
